## 9. Your Data Rights & Account Deletion

### 9.1 Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Export your data
- Object to data processing
- Withdraw consent at any time

### 9.2 How to Delete Your Account and Data

**Campaign Star** provides a simple way to permanently delete your account and all associated data.

#### Deletion Process:

1. **Visit our deletion page:**
   - Go to: [https://frontend-blush-seven-55.vercel.app/delete-account.html](https://frontend-blush-seven-55.vercel.app/delete-account.html)
   - This page is accessible without logging in

2. **Submit deletion request:**
   - Enter your registered phone number
   - Optionally provide your email address
   - Optionally tell us why you're leaving (helps us improve)
   - Confirm that you understand this action is permanent

3. **Verify with OTP:**
   - We'll send a one-time password (OTP) to your email (if provided)
   - Enter the OTP within 5 minutes to confirm deletion
   - This step prevents accidental or unauthorized deletions

4. **Deletion completed:**
   - Once confirmed, your data will be immediately and permanently deleted
   - You'll receive confirmation that the deletion was successful

#### What Gets Deleted:

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- ✅ **Your account:** Username, email, phone number, password, profile information
- ✅ **Your listings:** All advertisements and campaigns you created
- ✅ **Your images:** All photos uploaded for your listings (from our servers and cloud storage)
- ✅ **Your interactions:** Messages sent and received, favorites, reviews
- ✅ **Your activity:** Search history, activity logs, notifications
- ✅ **Your reports:** Any reports you submitted about other listings

#### Third-Party Services:

We store images on **Cloudinary** (cloud storage service). When you delete your account:
- We immediately request deletion of your images from Cloudinary
- Cloudinary typically processes deletions within 24 hours
- We cannot guarantee immediate deletion from third-party caches or backups

#### What We May Retain:

For legal and business purposes, we may retain certain information:
- **Transaction records:** If required by law (tax, fraud prevention) - typically 7 years
- **Aggregated data:** Anonymous statistics that cannot identify you
- **Legal obligations:** Data required for ongoing legal proceedings

#### Timeframe:

- **Immediate:** Account and personal data deleted from active databases
- **Within 24 hours:** Images deleted from Cloudinary cloud storage
- **Within 30 days:** Complete removal from all backups and archives

### 9.3 Alternative: Account Deactivation

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, contact us at **prathapv260@gmail.com** to:
- Temporarily deactivate your account
- Hide your listings without deleting them
- Export your data before deletion
